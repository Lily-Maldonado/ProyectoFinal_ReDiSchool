Breathing Trainer for Meditation

Instructions

Welcome to this meditation session. I'm glad to help you get involved in this path. After you choose a background sound, you can select the time that you would like to meditate. Once you select the time by pressing the button, the timer will start to run and give you 12 seconds to adopt a comfortable posture that allows you to focus on the breathing cycle. The first bell sound will indicate you to inhale for 4 seconds. When the second bell rings, hold your breath for 4 seconds, and when the drum sounds, exhale slowly in 4 seconds. Remember to take deep and slow breaths and feel and listen to yourself while you inhale and exhale through your nose. When your mind wanders, slowly return your attention to your breathing. GOOD LUCK!
